About Me

Currently, I am working as a Senior Research Associate at the University of Bristol, United Kingdom. My research primarily focuses on Optically active defects in semiconductors and their applications in a wide range of quantum technologies particularly quantum sensing.

Research Interests

  • Optically active defects
  • Integrated Quantum Photonics
  • Quantum Sensing
  • Devices fabrication and charecterization
