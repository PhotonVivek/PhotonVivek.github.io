About Me

I am a Senior Research Associate at the University of Bristol, United Kingdom. My research primarily focuses on optically active defects in semiconductors and their applications in quantum technologies, particularly quantum sensing.

Research Interests

  • Optically Active Defects
  • Integrated Quantum Photonics
  • Quantum Sensing
  • Device Fabrication and Characterization
