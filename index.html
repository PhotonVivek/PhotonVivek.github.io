About Me

I am a Senior Research Associate at the University of Bristol, United Kingdom. My research primarily focuses on Optically active defects in semiconductors and their applications in a variety of quantum technologies, particularly Quantum sensing .

Research Interests

  • Optically active defects: Single photon emitter
  • Integrated Quantum Photonics
  • Quantum Sensing
  • Device Fabrication
